Maque Choux

Description

Pronounced 'mock shoe', this is a corn dish.

Ingredients

  • the Holy Trinity - bell pepper, onion, and celery
  • CORN
  • andouille
  • chicken stock
  • seasonings

Steps

  1. Saute the sausage
  2. Chop your trinity. I usually do a 2:2:1 ratio of bell pepper:onion:celery$
  3. Saute your trinity in butter, just enough to soften the veggies.
  4. Add corn, garlic, and jalapenos.
  5. Add chicken stock.
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste, plus a bay leaf, cayenne to taste.
  7. Add chicken meat and andouille.
  8. Bring to a boil, then simmer.
  9. Eat!
