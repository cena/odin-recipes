Red Beans & Rice

Description

Traditionally, this dish was made on Mondays, since it could cook for hours while you did your laundry. (Mondays being laundry days in ye olde New Orleans.)

Ingredients

  • the Holy Trinity - bell pepper, onion, and celery
  • andouille sausage or ham hock
  • Camellia red beans
  • garlic
  • flour and oil for roux
  • seasonings

    • Steps

    1. Rinse beans.
    2. Saute sausage
    3. Chop your trinity. I usually do a 2:2:1 ratio of bell pepper:onion:celery
    4. Saute your trinity in butter, just enough to soften the veggies.
    5. Add beans, water, and bay leaf
    6. Bring to a boil, then simmer.
    7. Pour over rice. Eat!
